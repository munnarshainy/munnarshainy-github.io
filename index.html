About

Researcher, advocate,
curious mind.

I am a graduate of BSc Psychology Honours from CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bannerghatta Road Campus, Bengaluru. I recently adjourned my role as a Research Assistant at a US-Bengaluru-based BCI startup currently in stealth mode.

I was a Research Assistant for the MB4: Social Evaluation project of the ManyBabies4 network, led by Dr. Kelsey Lucca, Francis Yuen, and Dr. J. Kiley Hamlin, under Dr. Yiyi Wang (Post-doctoral Fellow, University of Chicago). I was also a remote Research Assistant at Stanford's Psychophysiology Lab under Kate Petrova, PhD Candidate.

Until 2024, I was at Axxonet Brain Research Laboratory, Bengaluru, as a full-time Research Intern — learning EEG and conducting research on neural markers of embodied emotion recognition, mood and emotion experiences, and remedial therapies including neurofeedback and brain function therapy (BFT).

I am eager to contribute to thoughtful, science-driven products — especially in neurotech, healthtech, mental health, and early-stage research-heavy startups that value rigour, curiosity, and humane ambition.

Outside research, I advocate for diversity and inclusion, mental health awareness, and queer rights. I enjoy drawing portraits and recently began reading more books.
